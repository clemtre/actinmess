Don Quixote, 2024,

Online version available at actinmess.com/donquixote.pdf

Stimulated by a concern for catastrophic ecological urgency and a frustration with urban space as a study environment, this writing is rooted in one term: ecovillages. Expressing a concrete and pragmatic will, seeking a material solution, this paper is focused on rurality, techniques and tools. Looking for a place to land and grow an alternative out of it, the label “ecovillages” came. This work looks at ecovillages in France and Netherlands, their relationship to tools and ecology and the position of architecture within this realm. Master thesis from the geo-design departement; the conclusion and the research sparked the project Site Drawing.

Anton W.V. Ripon is a spatial designer, researcher and production specialist, graduated from Ecole Duperré -FR and from the Design Academy of Eindhoven -NL. He works within interior design, exhibition design, landscaping, teaching, publication and research. Recently based in Belgium, he is open for commission work, collaboration and to assist on longterm studio work. He worked with summerschools -provisional school for Nothing, hackers&designers, domaine de Boisbuchet, private clients, writting contributions -the aquarium is a listening glass, revue cabane, light design -studio dogtooth.
